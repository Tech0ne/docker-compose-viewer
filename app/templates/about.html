About Docker Compose 3D Viewer

Visualize your Docker Compose clusters in a whole new dimension.

This tool transforms your docker-compose.yml setups into a fully interactive 3D visual environment. You can rotate, zoom, and inspect the relationships between services in real time, making it easier to debug and plan your infrastructure.

Built with Three.js for 3D rendering and powered by Bulma for a sleek, responsive UI, this viewer is designed for developers who like their ops data to look as good as it works.
